About Me

The engineer behind the numbers

I'm Christopher Denson — a Senior Manufacturing and Process Engineer with 8+ years of hands-on experience leading process optimization, scrap reduction, custom fixture design, and digital transformation in high-precision manufacturing environments including aerospace and high-volume plastics machining.

My strength is turning shop-floor problems into scalable systems. I've delivered $240,000+ in documented annual savings through systematic root cause analysis, Lean Six Sigma DMAIC projects, and process standardization. I design and build custom fixtures. I implement SPC-based quality control systems. And when the commercial tools don't exist or cost too much, I build them from scratch.

I taught myself Python, Streamlit, SQL, and machine learning on the job — building a custom AI-powered production analytics platform now used daily on the shop floor. I replaced $6,000 in commercial warehouse software with a custom app I built myself. That combination of deep floor credibility and modern data tools is what I bring to every engagement.

Currently pursuing my Bachelor of Engineering in Industrial Engineering Technology at Kennesaw State University. Open to Senior Manufacturing Engineer, Process Engineer, CI Engineer, and Industrial Engineer roles — remote, hybrid, or Atlanta area.
